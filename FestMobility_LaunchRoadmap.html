RTA Mandatory Features

  • Real-time trip reporting — data to RTA Road Management System (RTA API)

Development Options

Custom build: Full native iOS + Android. 6–10 weeks. AED 60,000–100,000. More control, harder to maintain.

Month 2
DET Mainland Operating Permit
Required to invoice mainland UAE companies · Start Month 1 with DIFC-only clients (free zone to free zone — no permit) · Apply Month 2
Why It's Needed
Executive Council Resolution No. 11 of 2025 requires free zone companies to hold a DET permit to conduct commercial activities with mainland UAE entities. Most hospitals, law firms, and embassies outside DIFC are mainland entities.
Month 1 Strategy — No Permit Needed
Focus your first B2B clients exclusively on DIFC-registered companies (free zone to free zone = unrestricted). DIFC hosts 600+ financial firms, law firms, and multinational offices. No permit needed for any of them.
